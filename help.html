Applying to Zorn

To apply to Zorn, visit our Apply page and select the position you're interested in. Fill out the application form with your information, social media links, and any required clips or portfolio links. Make sure to read the requirements for your chosen position before applying.

We have several positions available:

  • Freestyler - Showcase your Rocket League freestyle skills
  • Competitive Player - Join our competitive roster
  • Content Creator - Create engaging Rocket League content
  • Editor - Edit videos for our team and members
  • Designer - Create graphics, thumbnails, and branding
  • Coach - Help train and improve our players
  • Management - Assist with team operations and organization

Application review typically takes 3-7 business days. You'll receive a notification via the email you provided in your application. During peak periods, this may take slightly longer. If you haven't heard back within 2 weeks, feel free to contact us.

Yes! You can submit separate applications for different positions if you're qualified for multiple roles. However, make sure each application is tailored to the specific position and includes relevant experience and examples for that role.

After submission, your application will be reviewed by our leadership team. If selected, you'll be contacted via email or Discord for a potential interview or trial period. Even if you're not selected, we appreciate your interest and encourage you to apply again in the future!

Requirements & Eligibility

General requirements include:

  • Active Discord account
  • Professional and respectful attitude
  • Commitment to the team and its goals
  • Position-specific skills (varies by role)
  • Willingness to collaborate with team members

Rank requirements vary by position:

  • Freestyler: No minimum rank, but strong mechanical skills required
  • Competitive Player: Typically Champion 2 or higher
  • Coach: Grand Champion or higher recommended
  • Content/Creative Roles: No rank requirement

We welcome applicants of all ages! However, if you're under 13, you'll need parental consent to join our Discord server and participate in team activities, in accordance with Discord's Terms of Service.

Prior team experience is helpful but not required! We value talent, dedication, and the willingness to learn. If you have the skills for the position and a positive attitude, we encourage you to apply regardless of past team experience.
